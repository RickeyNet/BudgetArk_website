YOUR DATA, YOUR DEVICE

Privacy Policy

BudgetArk collects nothing. Everything you enter stays on your device.

BudgetArk
Effective Date: March 8, 2026
Developer: Rickey Cornett

Overview

BudgetArk is a personal finance app designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers.

Data Collection

BudgetArk does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No email addresses, phone numbers, or real names are collected
  • No location data is accessed or recorded
  • No usage analytics or tracking is performed
  • No advertising identifiers are used
  • No cookies or web tracking technologies are employed
  • No data is sent to any server, API, or third-party service

Data Storage

All data you enter into BudgetArk (including debts, payments, budget entries, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it using the "Export My Data" feature.

User Identity

BudgetArk generates a random anonymous identifier (UUID) on first launch. This identifier is stored only on your device and is not linked to any personal information. No account creation, sign-up, or login is required.

Data Export

The app includes an optional "Export My Data" feature that allows you to share your data as a JSON file through your device's native share sheet. This action is entirely user-initiated, and the exported data is handled by your device's operating system and whichever app or service you choose to share it with. BudgetArk does not process, intercept, or retain any exported data.

Third-Party Services

BudgetArk does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud storage providers.

Children's Privacy

BudgetArk does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, children are not asked to provide personal information.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by using the "Reset All Data" option in the app's Profile screen, or by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Since BudgetArk does not collect contact information, users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
